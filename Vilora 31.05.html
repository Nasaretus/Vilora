How It Works

We make donating easy. Just schedule a pickup and leave your items at the door. Our team will take care of everything — sorting, cleaning, and distributing where help is needed most.

If you have donations, simply send an SMS to 403-923-1990 with the word Vilora, or contact us via email at yourname@yourdomain.com, Facebook, or Instagram. Our team will reach out to arrange the best conditions and most convenient time to collect your items.

Step 1: Our team will pick up your unwanted items right from your doorstep.

Step 2: We will sort or repair the items you’ve donated to direct them toward various projects.

Step 3: The items you no longer needed will go on to benefit others.

About Us

We are a community-driven organization dedicated to doing good and giving unwanted items a second life.

We collect clothing, shoes, toys, books, sports equipment, and other items that people are ready to part with — so they can be passed on to those who truly need them.

Our team carefully sorts, restores, and redirects these items to where they can bring joy, support, and a sense of dignity.

But our mission goes beyond that. We run a variety of projects focused both on social support and on promoting environmental responsibility. We care about nature, animals, and children — and we work actively to reduce waste and raise awareness about conscious living.

Some of the resources we receive are reinvested into expanding our organization, allowing us to create even more opportunities to help those in need.

Doing good has never been easier. Just pack your unwanted items into the bag we leave at your door. One of our team members will pick it up — no hassle, no waiting.

We take care of the rest: sorting, repairing, recycling, and distributing the items through meaningful projects that make a real difference.

What We Accept

  • Clothing and shoes (in wearable condition)
  • Sports gear and equipment
  • Small household electronics
  • Dishes, kitchen utensils, and cookware
  • Books, toys, and creative supplies
  • Decorations, framed art, and posters
  • Bedding, sheets, blankets
  • Board games, DVDs, CDs, and other media
  • Perfumes, accessories, costume jewelry
  • Craft materials and leftover DIY supplies
  • Small appliances and hobby equipment
  • Bicycles, scooters, and recreational items
  • Unique miscellaneous items that could be useful to others
  • If you're unsure — just ask us. We're happy to help.

What We Cannot Accept

  • Large furniture (wardrobes, wall units, etc.)
  • Refrigerators, washing machines, stoves
  • Oversized or built-in items

If you're not sure whether we can accept your items, feel free to contact us at 403-923-1990.
Our team will be happy to assist you and arrange a convenient pickup time.
